pogi typings

Pogi typings, alam ang pinagkaiba ng at , mona at muna, nang at ng, din at rin. Hindi ginagawang "" ang "ko", "gani2" ang "ganito", "bak8" ang "bakit". It is fine to use short terms too (like btw, abt, tbh, hbu, tnx) but not every word need to be shorten, kasi yung iba hindi nila naiintindihan o nababasa. Pagdating naman sa tawa it doesn't matter kung o hindi. Knows how to use right punctuation marks in every sentences. Starts in big letter and nagtatapos sa tuldok, if walang subject it starts in small letter. Pogi typings doesn't mean you need to add a period every word you say.
